Matchmaker

Matchmaker determines whether you and I are meant for each other. You will be presented with 5 statements. Respond to them with a number 1-5. If you strongly disagree, respond with a 1. Respond with a 2 is you disagree. If you neither agree nor disagree, respond with a 3. Respond with a 4 is you agree, and 5 if you strongly agree.

Did Cupid stike us with an arrow, or did he miss? Use Matchmaker to find out...
